Our flaggers are trained and certified to PennDOT standards and equipped to direct traffic safely around highway, paving, and utility work. Whether it's a single-lane closure on a rural route or a multi-crew setup on a busy corridor, our teams arrive prepared, communicate clearly, and keep both motorists and the work crew protected.

- Certified, PennDOT-trained flaggers
- Single and multi-lane closures
- Short-term and long-term work zones
- Coordination with prime contractors and crews
