Truck-mounted attenuators that absorb impact and shield crews from live traffic.
<!--more-->

Our attenuator (TMA) trucks provide a mobile barrier between traffic and the work zone, protecting crews during paving, sweeping, line painting, and other moving or short-duration operations. Each truck is operated by a trained driver and positioned according to the approved traffic control plan.

- Truck-mounted attenuators for mobile and stationary work
- Shadow-vehicle support for paving and maintenance crews
- Mounted arrow boards for clear lane guidance
